This project focused on creating a brand identity for Giga Café, a modern café offering all-day breakfast, lunch, beverages, and a kid's menu. The aim was to design an elegant but modern style for the cafe. 
I began developing colour schemes as well as working on illustrations for their logo.

# Menu design.
The main attention was the layout. Ensuring it was readable and well-organised. While also adding an elegant style to their menu.

# Packaging Design.
Branded takeaway packaging, including coffee cups, bags, and other materials. 

# Website Development.
Giga Cafe has plans to introduce online ordering. This aims to provide customers with faster service and convenience.
