This project focused on creating a brand identity for Giga Café, a modern café offering all-day breakfast, lunch, beverages, and a kid's menu. The aim was to design a elegant but modern style for the cafe. 
I began, devolping colour schemes as well as working on illustrations for their logo.
---
Menu design. The main attention was the layout. Ensuring it was readible and well-organised. While also adding an elegant style to their menu.
---
Website Develpment. Giga Cafe has plans to introduce online ordering. This aims to provide customers with faster service and convenience. 
---
Packaging Design. Branded takeaway pakaging, including coffee cups, bags, and other materials.
